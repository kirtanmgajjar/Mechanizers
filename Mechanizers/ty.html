         Indian Government, at all levels, announces Welfare Schemes for a cross section of the society from time to time. These schemes could be either Central, State specific or a joint collaboration between the Centre and the States. In this section, we have attempted to provide you an easy and single point access to information about several welfare schemes of the Government and their various aspects including eligible beneficiaries, types of benefits, scheme details etc.


          "Bihar govt. has launched Mukhyamantri Kanya Utthan Yojana 2020 (मुख्यमंत्री कन्या उत्थान योजना) as a major initiative to stop child marriage and provide financial assistance to girls. This scheme has 3 components concerned with birth of girls, passing their inter examinations (मुख्यमंत्री बालिका (स्नातक) प्रोत्साहन योजना) and completion of graduation (मुख्यमंत्री बालिका (माध्यमिक+2) प्रोत्साहन योजना). All the girls will have to apply online for the last 2 components namely Mukhyamantri Balika Madhyamik +2 Protsahan Yojana & Mukhyamantri Balika Sanatak Protsahan Yojana. In this post, we will provide complete details of how to apply online, eligibility criteria and documents required. Under this scheme, govt. will provide Rs. 5000 on the birth of a girl child, Rs. 10000 after completion of 10th or 12th (+2) school examinations (unmarried) and Rs. 25000 on passing graduation degree. This ambitious girls welfare scheme will benefit around 1.6 crore girls per year. This scheme is applicable upto 2 girl child of a single family. Bihar govt. has started implementing Mukhyamantri Kanya Utthan Yojana from April 2019."

         To apply for the Pelli Kanuka, the applicant must follow the following eligibility criteria:-

The applicant must be a resident of Andhra Pradesh

An applicant must be above the age of 18 years
The annual household income of the applicant must be less than rupees 200000
The applicant must be a newlywed.
Marriage should have taken place in the state of Andhra Pradesh.
Widows and divorcees are not applicable to the scheme.

          The state govt. of Goa has launched 2 new schemes – PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) & Craft and Gear Scheme for fishermen. PMJJBY will provide insurance cover to the fishermen and crew against accidental death at sea. Craft and Gear Scheme will provide financial assistance on the purchase of tools for their trade. PMJJBY is an insurance scheme to enhance the adoption of Life Insurance Cover by the citizens. Fishermen have more chances of accidental death either due to drowning, rough weather conditions and natural calamities. So, govt. will provide compensation of Rs. 2 lakh to the victim’s family. Under Craft and Gear Scheme, govt. will provide subsidy ranging from 40% to 60% on the purchase of craft and gear as life support equipment.Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana for Fishermen PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana is a one year Insurance Scheme being offered by LIC. Any fishermen is now eligible to fill the PMJJBY Application / Claim Form and can get Rs. 2 lakh as compensation amount in case of death of the fishermen. However, the candidates must fulfill the eligibility criteria to become eligible to apply for PMJJBY:- PMJJBY Eligibility Criteria for Fishermen This scheme is applicable only for the fishermen who is already engaged in the occupation of fishing and other sea related activities. All the fishermen between the age of 18 to 50 years can apply. Applicants must be residing in Goa for the past 15 years or more. Fishermen must be a member of any of the Fisheries co-operative societies. Fishermen must submit his good health certificate and a self declaration that he is not suffering from any acute illness. Moreover all the fishermen must possess an active savings bank account for the auto debit facility of the premium amount. The annual premium of PMJJBY stands currently at Rs. 330. Out of this premium amount, 50% (Rs 165) will be borne by the Goa state government while the balance 50% will be borne by Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC).
